The 4 parts of the Diencephalon are:
1. Thalamus
2. EpiThalamus
3. SubThalamus
4. Hypothalamus

* The Diencephalon is a cluster of gray matter deep within the Cerebrum.
* The Thalamus is egg-shaped and the biggest part of the Diencephalon.
* The EpiThalamus is a tail-like structure, attached to the Thalamus.
  a. PinealGland
  b. Habenula
  c. PosteriorCommisure
* The SubThalamus is a brick-like structure we will get into later.
* The Hypothalamus makes of the remainder of the Diencephalon.
