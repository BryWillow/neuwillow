## [Diencephalon](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Diencephalon)  / A key part of the [Limbic System.](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Limbic_system)
The Diencephalon is a   cluster of gray matter deep within the Cerebrum. Its major components are:
- [Thalamus](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Thalamus)
- [Epithalamus](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Epithalamus)
    - [Pineal Gland](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pineal_gland)
    - [Habenula](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Habenula)
    - [Posterior Commisure](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Posterior_commissure)
- [Subthalamus](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Subthalamus)
- [Hypothalamus](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hypothalamus)
