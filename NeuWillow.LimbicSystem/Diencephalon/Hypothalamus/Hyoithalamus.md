## Hypothalamus: Purpose, Structure, Behavior Control

- Body temperature
- Hunger, Thirst
- Sleep, Fatigue
- Circadian Rhythms
- Parenting and maternal attachment
- Behaviors: Social, Sexual, Aggressiveness
